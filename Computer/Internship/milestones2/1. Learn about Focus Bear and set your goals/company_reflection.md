Understanding Focus Bear’s Mission & Vision

## Why do you think Focus Bear was created?

Focus bear was created as a way for (primarily) people with ADHD or focusing issues, to help complete tasks and stay focused. It's CEO - Jeremy Nagel, made the app due to his own previous experiences with his ADHD diagnosis. During orientation, he mentioned hoe he had trouble finding healthy and effective ways to stay focused. Therefore, in an effort to help others with similar issues; Focus bear was created to help enrich the lifes of neurodivergent people in a  more productive life. 

## What problem is Focus Bear solving?

Currently, alot of productivity apps are tailored more towards neurotypical people. Whilst seeking therapy and coaching programs is also effective, it is relatively inaccessable to alot of people due to the high cost. Focus Bear is trying to solve this problem by providing a more holistic solution that allows for distraction blockers during the day, and guiding users through daily routines/habits. For example, configuring your own habits, blocking certain apps from distraction and doing intensive focus sessions. 

## Why do you think this mission is important?

Diagnosis of ADHD is becoming more common due to increased awareness. This could also be due to the increasingly distracting aspects of modern tehcnology. As more neurdivergent people and those with attention issues are open about their conditions, there needs to be more tools that are tailored specifically for them.
