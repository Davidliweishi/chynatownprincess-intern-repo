📝 Reflection

How do Agile ceremonies help with communication and alignment?

It provides dedicated, scheduled spaces for teams to debrief, update and reflect on their work progresses. 
By sharing progresses and challenges regularly, team members can create an open culture where everyone understands the scope of their team and whats going on. 
By enabling open communication, teams can solve, discuss and improve their project tasks and vision - whilst also making for good trust building and team bonding. 

Which ceremony do you think is most important for your role, and why?

Daily stand up - I think this is important because it allows for immediate communication regarding where you are with your work, what needs to be done, and how you can receive any assitance (which is important for the learning phase of an internship).
